<h2 style="text-align: center;">Ghana</h2>
Ghana became a trading society when the world demanded gold to mint coins. Caravans carried metals to and from Ghana across  the Sahara. Ghana could not support it's growing population. it fell to Islamic expansion.
<h2 style="text-align: center;">Zimbabwe</h2>
Zimbabwe was a walled city home to 20,000 people. It showed organization as the stones making the wall were standardized. The city was extremely wealthy due to gold and diamond deposits.
<h2 style="text-align: center;">East Coast</h2>
Ships would often travel between the East African Coast and India. Africa would trade Ivory, gold and slaves. Some Chinese vessels would reach the Coast. Culture blending occurred. A new language called Swahili emerged.
